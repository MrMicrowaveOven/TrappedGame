Welcome to my laboratory! My name is Professor Buttercup, and I could use some help organizing my tiles.
The tiles will fall from the right side. There's a grid on the left side. Click a square on the grid to place a tile there.
Notice that when you place a tile, it changes what's on the tile:
For ADDITION: The ANSWER to the problem will be placed on the grid.
For ELEMENTS: The SYMBOL of the element will be placed on the grid.
For GREEK: The GREEK LETTER will be placed on the grid.
For ROMAN: The NUMBER VALUE of the Roman Numeral will be placed on the grid.
Don't fill the grid up too much, or I won't have anywhere else to put my tiles!
So how do you make room for more tiles? Simple. If there are ever any two tiles next to each other (up, down, left, right) with the same value, BOTH tiles will disappear! Do this to make room for more tiles.
As time goes by the tiles will fall FASTER, so do arrange them carefully.
If you fall too far behind, or if the grid fills up completely, it's game over.
